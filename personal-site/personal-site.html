Backstories & Basics

Is this you?

Are you drowning in unorganized character sheets? Do you spend way too long looking through old documents just to realize you'd stored your backstories on a different platform? Have you gotten tired of scrolling through countless pictures to show someone else your design?

Well Look No Further!

The purpose of Backstories & Basics is to help you organize your characters in a clean and concise manner, keeping track of their stats, story, and anything else you want to include to flesh out their personality.

What can you do with Backstories & Basics?

You can look at my characters because this project is really just an idea rather than an actual product that can be used by anyone but me :)
